# Hakka Pomelo (客家金柚)

A warm, refined, Xiaohongshu-meets-Hakka-culture theme. Earthy golds meet vermillion reds on rice-paper whites. Created for the Hakka Pomelo AI Recommendation System.

## Visual Language

- **Cards**: 14–20px radius, 2px shadow with warm-tinted black, 3px lift on hover
- **Buttons**: 26px pill radius, gradient fill (primary→lighter), 0.25s spring transition
- **Seals**: 2px solid border, -3° rotation, uppercase letter-spacing, used for origin/certification
- **Background**: SVG fractal noise at 3% opacity over paper base → rice-paper texture
- **Decorations**: Large blurred semi-circles at page edges (ink-wash aesthetic), radial hero glow
- **Loading**: Skeleton shimmer (warm gradient) → staggered card fade-up with blur dissolve

## Best Used For

Lifestyle e-commerce, cultural heritage products, food/agriculture tech, Xiaohongshu-style "种草" content, Chinese New Year campaigns, regional specialty platforms.
